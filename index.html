Extinct Plants Around the World

In the past century, plants have been going through an important biodiversity crisis. Many species from isolated areas face extinction due to human activities. Despite actions to preserve these endangered species, the magnitude of threats to biodiversity continue to grow. Using data from the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN) , we've gathered over 500 extinct species.

Key questions explored
1. Where are extinct species clustered geographically?
2. What are the greatest threats to biodiversity?
3. What actions are being taken?
4. When did species go extinct?
